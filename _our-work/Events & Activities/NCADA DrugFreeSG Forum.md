<h3>2026 'Resilient Youth &amp; Families' Forum</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="NCADA Forum Group Photo" src="/images/forum26.jpg">
</div>
<p>On 18 April 2026, approximately 100 participants from across Singapore's
anti-drug ecosystem came together at the NCADA DrugFreeSG 'Resilient Youth
&amp; Families' Forum. The programme included:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><a href="https://www.mha.gov.sg/media-room/newsroom/national-council-against-drug-abuse-ncada-drugfreesg-forum-2026/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Opening remarks</a> by
the Guest-of-Honour A/P Muhammad Faishal Ibrahim, Acting Minister-in-charge
of Muslim Affairs and Senior Minister of State, Ministry of Home Affairs</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Presentations on the <a href="https://www.ncada.org.sg/our-work/perception-surveys/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">National Drug Perception Survey 2025</a> and
the Study on Parent-Child Conversations by the Singapore University of
Social Sciences (SUSS)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>A panel discussion with NCADA members, Dr Victor Seah from SUSS, and DrugFreeSG
youth advocate Mr Dervin Lua</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Breakout group discussions where participants shared insights and solutions
that will strengthen community efforts in supporting youth and families
in drug prevention</p>
</li>
</ul>
